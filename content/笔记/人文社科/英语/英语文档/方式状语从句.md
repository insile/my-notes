##### 方式状语从句
- [[从属连词]]
	- as if/though
		- He acted as if he knew the answer.
		- 他的举止像是知道答案一样。
	- just as so 
		- Just as the sun rises in the east, so it sets in the west.
		- 正如太阳从东方升起，它也从西方落下。
	- in the way that
		- She explained the concept in the way that everyone could understand.
		- 她以大家都能理解的方式解释了这个概念。
